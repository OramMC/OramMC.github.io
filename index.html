About Me

Hello! I am a senior undergraduate at Sun Yat-sen University, majoring in Physics and Astronomy. I am conducting research under the supervision of Professor Yiming Hu, with research interests in Gravitational Wave Data Analysis.

I will go to the Department of Astronomy at Tsinghua University this August to pursue my Ph.D. degree. Currently, I am completing my undergraduate thesis, conducting research on pulsar searching under the supervision of Professor Dongzi Li at Tsinghua University.
